Dear Iris Families, 


This week we see children making more connections with each other and showing greater interaction with both old and new friends. We are happy to see them caring for one another, offering help, and demonstrating how to do different tasks in the classroom. 

Children's self-portrait now are displayed on the wall. They enjoy looking at their own work and their friends' as well.

Dance Class

This week the children had their first dance class. We are going to have a kungfu class in the future too. 
Small group learning

In this week's small group learning the children practiced safely handling scissors, putting fingers into the holes accordingly and cutting line, rectangle, zigzag and oval shape on paper. They called the zigzag 'crocodile teeth' and the oval 'dinosaur egg'; We also explored some letters with playdoh. Some preferred to use a long 'noodle' to form a letter, and others liked to break down the letter with several pieces before forming. It was so nice to see the children help each other to solve the problems during the task.
