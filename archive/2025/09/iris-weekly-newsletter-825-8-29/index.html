Exploring the new environment and material

This week, our children were busy exploring the new classroom, adjusting to the daily routines and the environment. We particularly focused on two areas during our small group time: Calming area and Using water color in the art area. 

  In the calming area, the children found and learned some tools that can help us to regulate our emotions. They are lavender bag, sand and oil timer, sensory squeeze toy, ear muffler.  

  In the art area, the children learned how to get water by themselves, how much water should be put in the cup, where they can find brushes in different sizes and how to keep the color clean and not mixed with other colors. Most of all, they learned that they can come to make art work freely and independently.
